Our Services

Buyers Inspection

This is the home inspection that everyone needs to get when they are buying a home. It includes a point by point examination of the home particularly focusing on Roofing, Exterior, Structure, Insulation, Ventilation, Heating, Cooling, Electrical, Plumbing and Interior.

Seller Inspection

Having a pre-home assessment can be a major advantage if you are planning for repairs. You can do the repairs on your termsâ€”not the buyers. For example if your home inspector finds a leak under the sink, you can fix it by your own way to save a lot of money.

Mold Inspection

Mold is just one of the many issues that affect the air we breathe. We do inspection of the structure to determine its current condition relative to the possibility of fungal contamination. It's good to inspect through our services mold inspection and swab testing.

Radon Inspection

The colorless, odorless and radioactive gas that seeps up from the earth and is the second leading cause of lung cancer in Canada. Protect yourself now by testing your home, reducing your exposure and protecting your loves ones. Be sure to check your home this winter for Radon.

Asbestos Testing

Asbestos is a naturally occurring mineral composed of thin fibers. Its been used in insulation, fireproofing, and other construction materials. The environmental protection agency has began regulating asbestos containing. Asbestos has been found to pose serious health risk.

Air Quality Test

Research describes that we now spend up to 90% of our time indoors- sleeping, eating, working and socializing. Indoor pollution sources that release gases or particulates into the air are the primary cause of indoor air quality problems in homes and that can be inspected by our air quality inspection.
